About Me

Derek Johnson is the founder of Whirlybug Productions, a B2B company which provides drone solutions to real estate management companies. In turn, realtors can showcase their properties and better market their listings to buyers while offering sellers a cutting-edge presentation of their property. Prior to founding Whirlybug Productions, Johnson worked as a specialized Internet Sales Manager at Coggin Automotive Group, one of the largest automobile retailers in the United States with an annual overturn of some 180,000 retail vehicles and revenues of $6.5 billion. Here, he oversaw a team which offered both new and used cars from popular auto brands including Chevrolet, Buick, Toyota, and other domestic and foreign marques, successfully closing deals with clients. Johnson is also an expert PC technician and has worked several positions in the PC Repair industry including Manager of Command Computers, where he helped clients to fix hardware issues with their PCs. Prior to this, he worked as a Technical Installer at Groupware International – GI Broadband, providing both residential and commercial clients with the telecommunications they required successfully through expert cable installation and network integration services. Johnson is also a helicopter pilot who has attended flight school and recently became a father to a young boy. His passion for technology began when he was a young boy, tinkering with his parents’ computer files to make programs run despite the limited RAM (Random Access Memory) that it had – from this grew his fascination for the hardware behind the software, and a natural entry into careers where this passion was rewarded. Currently, Johnson is attending Georgia Tech Professional Education’s Coding Boot Camp to hone his skills as a web development professional and further enhance the portfolio of value he can offer to his clients in drone technology, web development, and sales skills.
